    Welcome to the Novena

    Novena is public donation center with blood and Organ donation members in the changing health care system. Novena is one of the nation's oldest and largest nonprofit transfusion organizations. We provide a blood-Organ and volunteer services across the India. With our national footprint, deep community roots and specialized services, we are the thread that connects people and resources together to fuel progress in transfusion medicine.

    • Inspiring people to give blood and Organs.
    • Specialist Blood, Organ donors and clinical supervision.
    • Producing a safe and ready blood supply.
    • Increasing communication with our members.
    • High quality assessment, diagnosis and treatment.
    • Offering specialized patient services and pharmaceuticals.
